Tulips

A Symbol of Beauty, History, and Springtime

Botanical Characteristics

  • Scientific Name : Tulipa
  • Family : Liliaceae
  • Native Regions : Central Asia, Iran, and Turkey
  • Bloom Time : Early to late spring
  • Colors : Red, pink, yellow, white, purple, orange, and multi-colored varieties

Introduction

Tulips are among the most beloved and recognizable flowers in the world. With their vibrant colors,
elegant shape,and rich cultural history, tulips have captivated gardeners, artists, and nature lovers for centuries.
They are especially associated with spring, symbolizing renewal, beauty, and hope.
